Grow Your Own Pineapple

Growing your own pineabpple at home can be fun and educational for the whole family. They are very versitle plants and can even be grown indoors if you don't live in a tropical climate.

What you will need:
  • ❀ A whole pineapple with green healthy leaves.
  • ❀ Catus Soil
  • ❀ Planting pot large enough to hold a whole pinapple.
Step 1: When removing the fruit cut top off evenly.
Step 2: After adding soil to the planter. Place freshly cut pineapple-top directly on soil.
Step 3: Water and mist your pineapple once a week. Do not let the plant get overly wet and soggy.
